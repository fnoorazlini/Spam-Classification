📌 What is a Spam Message?

Spam messages refer to unsolicited or irrelevant communications sent to a large number of recipients, often for advertising, phishing, or fraudulent purposes. These messages may appear across various platforms, including SMS, email, and messaging applications.

Common characteristics of spam messages include:

  • 🔗 Suspicious or deceptive hyperlinks
  • 💸 Promises of financial rewards or prizes
  • 📣 Excessive or irrelevant advertisements
  • 📥 Requests for sensitive personal information

Users are advised to avoid interacting with unknown messages, refrain from clicking unfamiliar links, and utilize spam filters or classification systems to enhance digital safety.

This classification system aims to assist in identifying and filtering out potential spam to improve user experience and online security.
